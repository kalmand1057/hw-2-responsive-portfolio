About Me

My name is Kalena Almand and I was born and raised here in San Antonio, TX. I am 24 years old and some of my hobbies include hiking, reading, cooking and swimming. The first time I interacted with coding, I didn't even realize it! During the early stages of social media, I would modify my page's colors, backgrounds, font, and anything else I could figure out in the code for my page. It's only been about 3 weeks that I've learned the basics of coding, but the more I learn, the more I appreciate how far coding has come since then and how much we take it for granted in this day and age. As for the future, I feel there is so much more every day technology can do for us to make our day to day easier, and I would love to be a part of it!
